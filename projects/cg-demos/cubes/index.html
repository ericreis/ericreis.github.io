CUBE CONTROLS:
  • Left click on empty space to create new cube.
  • Left/Right click on existing cube to select it.
  • Left click and move mouse to translate selected cube.
  • Right click and move mouse to rotate selected cube.
  • Select cube and press "D" button to delete the cube.
CAMERA CONTROLS:
  • Left click and move mouse to rotate the camera.
  • Right click and move mouse to translate the camera.
  • Scroll up and down to zoom in and zoom out.
